The Concept

Like Paint & Sip,
but you build actual gadgets.

You've seen Paint and Sip. Same idea — except instead of a canvas, you leave with a working piece of technology you built with your own hands. All in the company of good people and good drinks.

🔧

Guided from Start to Finish

Every step is walked through by an instructor. You won't get lost, and you won't need a engineering degree.

🍺

At Your Favorite Bar

We host at local venues. Order your drink, find your seat, and get to work.

⚡

Take It Home

First Build

Solar-Powered
Bitcoin Miner

Your first Nerd 'n Sip project: a fully functional solo Bitcoin miner powered by a solar cell and rechargeable battery. Built around the ESP32 microcontroller — one of the most capable tiny computers on the planet.

  • ⚙️ ESP32 microcontroller
  • 🔋 18650 rechargeable battery + charging circuit
  • ☀️ Solar cell
  • 📺 Small display showing live mining stats

No soldering. No coding experience required. Pre-flashed and ready to assemble.
